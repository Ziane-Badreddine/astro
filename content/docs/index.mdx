## Introduction

Welcome to **astro** — your destination for modern programming education. We believe in learning by doing, which is why every tutorial, crash course, and guide is designed to get you coding from day one.

Whether you're taking your first steps in programming or leveling up your existing skills, astro provides **practical, hands-on tutorials** that mirror real-world development. No fluff, no outdated content — just clear, actionable lessons that help you build actual projects.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Who is astro for?">
    astro is designed for anyone who wants to learn programming — from complete beginners starting their coding journey to experienced developers looking to master new frameworks and technologies. Our content is structured to meet you wherever you are in your learning path.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are the tutorials really free?">
    Yes! All tutorials, crash courses, and learning materials on astro are completely free. We believe quality programming education should be accessible to everyone, with no paywalls or hidden costs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What programming languages do you cover?">
    We focus on the most in-demand languages for modern development: **JavaScript**, **Python**, **C++**, and **Java**. We also cover popular frameworks like React, Node.js, Django, and essential tools like REST and GraphQL APIs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are the tutorials structured?">
    Each tutorial follows a practical, hands-on approach. You'll start with core concepts, then immediately apply them through code examples and real projects. We prioritize learning by doing over pure theory.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between tutorials and crash courses?">
    **Tutorials** are comprehensive, in-depth guides that cover topics thoroughly with detailed explanations. **Crash courses** are condensed, fast-paced lessons designed to get you up and running quickly — perfect for learning the essentials fast.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need prior programming experience?">
    No prior experience is required for our beginner content. We have learning paths for all skill levels — from your very first line of code to advanced architectural patterns used by senior developers.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often is the content updated?">
    We regularly update our tutorials to reflect the latest versions, best practices, and industry standards. Our tech blog keeps you informed about new releases and changes in the ecosystem.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I suggest topics or request tutorials?">
    Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. If there's a topic you'd like us to cover or a tutorial you need, reach out through our contact page or community channels.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
